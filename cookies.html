What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your cookie consent preferences
  • Ensure the website functions properly
  • Understand how visitors use our website (if you consent to analytics cookies)

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like remembering your cookie preferences. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

We'd like to use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve the website and provide a better user experience. These cookies are only set if you click "Accept" in our cookie notice.

Currently, we do not use any analytics cookies, but we may implement them in future with your consent.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Through Our Website

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie notice at the bottom of the page. You can choose to:

  • Accept: Allow all cookies (including future analytics cookies)
  • Reject: Only allow essential cookies needed for the website to work

If you change your mind, you can clear your browser's cookies and revisit our website to see the notice again.

Through Your Browser

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. However, if you block all cookies (including essential ones), some parts of our website may not work properly.

To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org

Third-Party Cookies

We do not currently use any third-party cookies on our website. If this changes in future, we will update this policy and seek your consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
