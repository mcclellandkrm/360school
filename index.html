Common Questions

Everything principals ask before getting started

How technical is this for my staff?

Not at all. We handle everything - shooting the 360° tour, building the website, and setting it up. You provide access to your school and feedback. We deliver a finished product that works immediately. No technical knowledge required from your team.

How quickly can this be delivered?

Virtual Tour: 2-3 weeks from shoot to delivery
Website: 4-6 weeks from kickoff to launch
Website + Tour: 6-8 weeks total
We schedule around your calendar and work efficiently to minimize disruption.

What's included in the virtual tour?

Professional 360° photography of your key spaces (classrooms, halls, facilities, outdoor areas), interactive hotspots with information, navigation between rooms, mobile-optimized viewing, and hosting on our platform. You get a link to embed on your site or share directly.

Do I need to manage hosting or updates?

We handle initial hosting setup and handover complete access. For ongoing updates, you can manage simple content changes yourself (we'll show you how), or we offer optional maintenance packages. The tour hosting is included permanently - no annual fees.

What if we need changes after launch?

Essential package includes 3 months support, Complete package includes 6 months. During this time, we fix any issues and make reasonable adjustments at no extra cost. After support period, we offer pay-as-you-go updates or optional maintenance plans.

Can parents view this on their phones?

Absolutely. Everything we build is mobile-first. The virtual tours work seamlessly on phones, tablets, and computers. Parents can explore your school from any device, anywhere, anytime. No apps or downloads required.
