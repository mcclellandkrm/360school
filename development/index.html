In Development

Active Testing

Gaussian Splatting

A breakthrough in 3D capture that turns ordinary footage into photorealistic, navigable environments. Walk through a school corridor with depth and detail that traditional 360° photography can't match — captured with a single camera pass.

Active Testing

360° Video

Immersive video walkthroughs that bring school life to life — assemblies, sports days, classroom moments. Prospective families experience the energy and atmosphere of a school, not just its walls. Embeds directly into virtual tours.

Evaluating

New Camera Hardware

The latest generation of 360° cameras, drone-mounted rigs and multi-lens systems are being evaluated for image quality, workflow and value. Better hardware means sharper tours, faster production and lower cost to schools.

Early Research

Software & Integration

New virtual tour platforms, AI-assisted editing tools and interactive overlay systems are being tested. The goal is richer experiences — think voice narration, animated hotspots, parent portal integrations and accessibility features built in from day one.
